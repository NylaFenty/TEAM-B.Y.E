About.

We, Traci Harvey and Lisa Murry, started Goddess of the Cosmos because we love the earth and all things in and on it. We both are Reiki practitioners; Lisa, the is a Reiki Master and Tracy is also a Master Cosmetologist. We are dear friends and have known each other for more than 25 years. We wanted something more than what was currently available for healthy skincare. We wanted to know what we were putting on our precious skin, the biggest organ of the human body. We wanted something natural that was truely ALL natural. Zero chemicals!! Nothing synthetic!!

We didn't want to use chemical, hazardous plastics to store something intended to 'feed' our skin. We found wonderful glass jars and bottles. We wanted things free of synthetic chemicals. We looked for and found the highest quality ingredients. We wanted to see and feel the difference in our skin. We wanted to use things that are better for the environment; Buying, from businesses that are making a difference in mulitple ways. As little plastic and waste as possible, gmo free, fair trade, natural, pure, unrefined, wild crafted, organic goodness. We loved what we created, friends and family noticed the difference and soon we began getting requests to make products available to others!! Turns out we weren't the only ones that wanted safe and nourishing skin care!! Goddess of the Cosmos was born! Be sure to follow us on Facebook!
